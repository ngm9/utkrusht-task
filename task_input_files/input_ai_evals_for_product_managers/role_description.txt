As a Founding Product Lead, you will work closely with engineering, business, founders, and growth teams. You will connect insights across teams and turn them into products that ship fast and create impact.

We are looking for someone who has worked in a startup environment, understands ownership deeply, and can operate independently without constant direction.



What You Will Do
Manage stakeholders across engineering, business, founders, and growth teams
Write clear and actionable product specifications for engineering
Define success metrics for product features and track performance
Spend time inside the product to understand evals, gaps, and user pain points
Work directly with founders and engineers with minimal hierarchy
Translate user insights into product improvements and new features
